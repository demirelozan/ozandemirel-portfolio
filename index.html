About

🎼

Ozan Demirel

Composer & Sound Designer

I'm a passionate composer specializing in creating emotional and immersive soundscapes for visual media. With a deep understanding of how music enhances storytelling, I craft unique sonic experiences that bring narratives to life across film, games, and advertising.

My approach combines traditional orchestral techniques with modern production methods, allowing me to create versatile soundtracks that range from intimate piano pieces to epic orchestral scores. Each project is an opportunity to explore new sonic territories and push creative boundaries.

Whether it's the subtle emotional undertones of a character's journey or the grand themes of an epic adventure, I believe music has the power to transform visual experiences into unforgettable moments.
